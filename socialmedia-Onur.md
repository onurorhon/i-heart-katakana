## Drafts

### LinkedIn

Had 67 categories in my katakana learning app. Users would have to scroll forever to find what they wanted.

Consolidated them down to 9 parent categories. Food, Animals, Technology, etc. Same words, way less cognitive load.

Also redesigned the menus as floating cards with small gaps between them. You can see the content peeking through behind. Tiny detail but it makes everything feel lighter.

Sometimes the best feature you can add is removing 58 options.
